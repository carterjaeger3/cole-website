Reading List

Spiritual Books I've Read

Books that have shaped Cole's faith, with a few thoughts on each.

Introduction to the Devout Life

St. Francis de Sales

Practical and gentle — a good companion for anyone trying to grow in holiness in the middle of a busy, ordinary life.

He Leadeth Me

Fr. Walter Ciszek, S.J.

A hard, honest account of finding God's presence in suffering. Reread this before every mission trip.

The Screwtape Letters

C.S. Lewis

Sharp, funny, and uncomfortably accurate about the small ways we talk ourselves out of holiness.
